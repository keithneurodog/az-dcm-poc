  Overview, Key Challenge, Opportunity
  -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Overview:  Enhance data & AI driven decision making across the R&D pipeline through delivery of new open access paradigms (90:10), expanded pools of readily available data, and smarter more scalable data sharing patterns Key Challenge:  To operate at the speed and scale required to achieve our 2030 Bold Ambition AZ must uplift its core data & AI capability. Transforming R&D's ability to share & access data will be a key enabler in this space -- including better and faster internal data sharing, collaboration with external partners, AI utilisation, and compliance / regulatory requirements. Opportunity:  Uplifting R&D's ability to share and access AZ's proprietary data will be a core competitive advantage in the industry. Additionally, leveraging external data and partnerships effectively will drive continued innovation across the pipeline.
