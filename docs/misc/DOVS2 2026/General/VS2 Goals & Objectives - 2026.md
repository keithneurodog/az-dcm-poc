# *Value Stream Goals & Objectives 2026*

## **VS2 General / Overall**

*Simplified & EDE -- R&D's priority AI needs are met for patient
clinical data...*

*Goal: Embed an AI-enabled, AI-ready data sharing and access paradigm
across R&D -- scaling 90:10 for priority Quantitative Scientists (10
groups, \>1,000 users); delivering 5 key steps towards a new vision for
external data sharing; achieving a step-change in the value of R&D's
patient data asset (\>1,700 new & legacy studies AI-ready); and
deploying agentic automation for access (Collectoid) and discovery
(Sherlock).*

## **WP1 - 90:10 Data Access**

> *Goal: By end of Q4, operationalise the 90:10 principle for priority
> Quantitative Scientist groups across at least **10 R&D teams** (and
> **\>1,000 users**) - with ≥90% of user data needs fulfilled via open
> access models (ROAM, Broad Build, Data Product Access) and ≤10% via
> streamlined requests (IDAP)*

- **ROAM Rollout Phase 1**

*Goal: By end of Q2, achieve full ROAM rollout to Oncology and Biopharma
highest priority users across five key groups (ODSAI, Early Oncology,
Biometrics BP, Biometrics ONC, EAI), supporting \>500 users with open
access*

### **ROAM Rollout Phase 2**

*Goal: By end of Q4, complete ROAM rollout Phase 2 to five additional
groups (Patient Safety/TRISARC, TSEM, CPSS, Clinical Operations, Late
Clinical), supporting \>500 further users with open access and bringing
cumulative coverage to ≥10 groups and \>1,000 users*

### **Mature 90:10 Processes**

*Goal: By end of Q4, mature 90:10 access operations to best in class
data sharing standards: Q1 - publish ROAM v1 & current '10' processes
for internal audit; Q2 -- full hand-over of collections management for
Phase 1 users to DPO with process/tool uplifts; Q3 -- publish ROAM v2 &
improved '10' processes in BIC incorporating user feedback; Q4 -
transition E2E management of ROAM and enhanced '10' processes to DPO
BAU*
