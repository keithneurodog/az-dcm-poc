Government & Public Sources

We directly train our models on official government sources, including legislation, case law, and regulatory updates. This is either publicly available or accessed through our trusted relationships, ensuring a foundation of reliable, authoritative data.

2. Partnerships with Law Firms

We collaborate with leading law firms who are LEGALFLY clients or partners. Their anonymized data is used to enhance our training, capturing the nuances of real-world legal practice while fully safeguarding client confidentiality.

3. Publisher Integrations

LEGALFLY partners with leading legal publishers such as FromCounsel in the UK and Wolters Kluwer across Europe and the US. This provides our system with up-to-date access to premium legal commentary, new legislation, and case law.
