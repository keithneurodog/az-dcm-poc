## Value Stream 2 UI /Agentic AI Workshop Summary  {#slide-2}

The summary points in the following slides represent the consensus output from the VS2 agentic AI and UI workshops held in December 2025. 

The outputs in their current state, do not constitute a comprehensive or final scope for the DOVS2 2026 WP4 deliverables. 

Instead, they will serve as significant inputs to draft the scope proposal which will then be refined in collaboration with key team members .
