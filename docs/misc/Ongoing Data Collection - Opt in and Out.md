# Dataset review for inclusion or exclusion

We provide two options for review:

(1) Simple -- review by products and study codes/names only.

(2) Detailed -- review by product, study name and a more detailed reason
    why that particular study has been included.

The requirement (and result) of either review is the same, insofar as it
will either include, or exclude data from the Open Access Collection.

## Review -- Detailed Option

Below we show all studies requiring review for inclusion in this Open
Data Collection.

- The information is ordered by product and study.

- The study ID is also a link to AstraZeneca Clinical Trials to aid easy
  access to further study detail.

- Detailed criteria for inclusion criteria are also listed, using the
  following key:

<!-- -->

- **C1** - Oncology R&D's clinical study datasets (full primary) from
  closed (historical) clinical studies that have achieved DBL across all
  arms, but the latest DBL was \<6 months ago.

- **C2** - Oncology R&D's clinical study datasets (full primary) from
  ongoing studies that have stopped active recruitment across the entire
  study but remain in follow-up or monitoring phases and have achieved a
  DBL for reporting or analysis across all arms, but the latest DBL was
  \<6 months ago.

- **C3** - Oncology R&D's clinical study data-subsets (full primary) for
  individual study arms of actively recruiting studies, where those
  specific arms have been closed to enrolment, where the DBL for the arm
  was \<6m ago.

- **C4** - Oncology R&D's clinical study data-subsets (full primary) for
  individual study arms of actively recruiting studies, where those
  specific arms have been closed to enrolment, where the DBL for the arm
  was \>6m ago.

- **C5 -** Oncology R&D's clinical study datasets (full primary) from
  open-label phase 1 and 2 studies that have achieved FSI, but may or
  may not have achieved DBL, where participants and investigators are
  aware of the treatment assignment.
