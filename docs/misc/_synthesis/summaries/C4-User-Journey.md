## User Journey Steps `[ref: slide 2]`

### One-Off Steps

| Phase | Activities |
|-------|------------|
| **AWARENESS** | Contact email confirming inclusion; SharePoint page for comms; User Guide with training links |
| **COMPLIANCE** | Mandatory training; R&D Data Office performs provisioning |

### Repeatable Steps (Per Project)

| Phase | Activities | Key Questions Answered |
|-------|------------|------------------------|
| **IDEATION** | ROAM Dashboard; look for new tools | What collections do I have access to? What data is in them? |
| **PRE-PLANNING** | Self-review; check against User Guide | Is the data use aligned to collection terms? |
| **EXECUTION** | Access via PDP | How do I access the data I need? |
| **CLOSE DOWN** | Project Completion Form | How do I share outcomes compliantly? |

### Support

- SharePoint page (Transforming Data Sharing & Access)
- DPO team within R&D Data Office for individual assistance

---

## Key User Questions Answered `[ref: slide 2]`

1. How does Open Access work & what is a data collection?
2. What collections do I have access to and what data is in them?
3. What am I allowed to do with the data I have access to?
4. What training do I have to do to access data?
5. What sort of data will support our project?
6. Is the data use aligned to the collection terms?
7. How do I access the data that I need?
8. How do I share project outcomes in a compliant way?
9. What help documents can I refer to -- and how do I get help from Data Office?
