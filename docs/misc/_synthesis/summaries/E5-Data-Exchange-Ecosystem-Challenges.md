## Key Points

- **Topic:** Challenges in vendor data transfers and partner collaborations
- **Root Cause:** Gaps in contracting and consent reviews `[ref: slide 2]`
- **Impact:** Prolonged timelines, legal/regulatory risk, reduced efficiency, eroded collaborator experience

---

## Problem Statement `[ref: slide 2]`

AstraZeneca is experiencing recurring delays in vendor data transfers and partner collaborations due to:

1. **ICF not reviewed before contracting:** Risk of ingesting non-compliant data
2. **Contracts without essential data protection terms:** Post-signature amendments required
3. **Amendment execution delays:** Dependencies on teams with limited capacity
4. **Template and negotiation challenges:** Teams lack confidence in privacy/contract language
5. **Reliance on scarce specialist support:** Bottleneck

---

## Challenges by Category `[ref: slide 3]`

### Vendor Transfers - Incoming AZ Data
- Correct contracts not in place or hard to find
- Missing Data Protection Appendixes (DPAs) and Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
- CROs struggling with Clinical Site/Trial Agreements
- SCCs not completed correctly (missing info, TOMs dropped)
- Ingestion team struggling to identify procurement lead
- Coupa contracting processes are lengthy

### Vendor Transfers - Outgoing AZ Data
- Contracts lack correct DPAs/SCCs
- Struggling to find principal agreements
- SCCs incomplete or missing required appendixes
- Statement of Work doesn't contain full services scope

### Collaborations - Ingesting Data
- ICFs not reviewed prior to contracting
- DPA not aligned with ICF
- DPA lists wrong data protection roles
- Country of origin unclear (US data access restrictions)
- Missing SCCs where legally required

---

## Proposed Solutions `[ref: slide 4]`

### 1. Identify At-Risk Teams
Map and proactively engage teams involved in data-related contracting to provide early, targeted guidance

### 2. Establish Pre-Approved Vendor List
Partner with procurement to maintain vendors with vetted contracts containing:
- Privacy appendixes
- Standard Contractual Clauses
- Relevant due diligence

### 3. Review and Upgrade Contract Templates
Modernize templates to include correct DPAs and SCCs, closing known gaps

### 4. Provide Contract and Privacy Toolkits
- Checklists, playbooks, decision trees
- Targeted training for template selection and completion

### 5. Deploy AI-Legal Tools
Adopt AI legal tools (e.g., LEGALFLY) to:
- Streamline consent reviews
- Speed contract drafting and negotiation
- Stay current with evolving laws/regulations
- Scale consistent, high-quality reviews
- Reduce reliance on specialists
- Shorten support timelines
