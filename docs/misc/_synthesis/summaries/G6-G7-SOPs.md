## SOP-0108734: Management of Important New Safety Issues `[ref: G7]`

### Status:
- **Format:** PDF - not directly readable
- **Referenced by:** ROAM documentation, User Guides

### Known Content (from references):

**Purpose:** Defines requirements for managing safety signals discovered during data analysis

**Key Requirements:**
- 24-hour notification for medical/safety findings
- Process for reporting to safety teams
- Does NOT override safety signal processes `[ref: C1]`

**Training:**
- One of 4 mandatory trainings for ROAM users `[ref: C2, C5, C6]`
- Safety training (Course C in training matrix)

## Relationship to ROAM

These SOPs are foundational guardrails that ROAM operates under:

```
Global Standard (STND-0001498)
    ↓
SOP-0067196 (Internal Sharing)
    ↓
ROAM Guidance (C1)
    ↓
OAC Agreements & AOTs
```

**User Impact:**
- Users must complete SOP training before ROAM access
- All data use must comply with these SOPs
- Safety issues must be reported per SOP-0108734
