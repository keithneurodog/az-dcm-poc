## Known Content (from references in other documents)

Based on references in C1-C8:

- Sets out requirements for internal sharing of scientific data
- Establishes the Data Accountability Framework
- Defines Due Diligence Process for data sharing
- Requires:
  - Scientific Merit assessment
  - Contractual restrictions review
  - Legal and Ethical basis confirmation
  - Business Sensitivity consideration
- Establishes Data Protection Principles:
  - Data sub-setting
  - Secure environments
  - Legal protection obligations
- Requires sharing of insights from analysis
- Specifies GxP requirements for decision-making analyses
