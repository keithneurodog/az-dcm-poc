## Closed & Grey Zone Studies `[ref: Section 2]`

### Current Understanding:
1. Proactive gathering of closed studies clinical patient data from entimICE and CTDS
2. On-request addition of closed studies into PDP when data locations available
3. Collect IO (and other modalities) data for as many studies as possible

### Outstanding Questions:
1. What are current needs for studies outside entimICE and CTDS?
2. Need layer for getting data locations for missing studies in PDP request process

### Definition Questions:
- **Closed studies definition:**
  - CSR + 6 months?
  - Treatment extensions (likely with no CSR date)?
  - Closed arms for ongoing trials?
- **Scope definition:**
  - AZ studies?
  - Prohibited?
  - Non-entimICE/non-PDR studies?
