## Data Scope Criteria `[ref: Section 2.2]`

### Included:
- BioPharma AZ interventional studies across CVRM, V&I, R&I
- Studies classified as "Closed" (achieved final DBL > 6 months ago)
- Full patient population (Primary Use scope)

### Excluded:
- Studies with DBL < 6 months ago
- Ongoing studies not yet achieved final DBL
- Non-AZ Sponsored studies
- Non-interventional studies

### Data Types Included:
| Category | Types | Status |
|----------|-------|--------|
| Study & Patient Data | Individual patient level data, study metadata | Included |
| Data Modalities | eCRF, digital devices, medical imaging, molecular/'omic data | Included |
| Data Formats | DICOM, SDTM, FASTQ, CSV, other | Included |
| Geographies | Data outside China not requiring HGR approval | Included |

## Opt-in/Opt-out Process `[ref: Section 2.2]`

**Quarterly Cadence:**
1. **Quarterly time-point:** Data Office prepares list of data for inclusion
2. **Prepare lists:** Identify candidates for opt-in/opt-out decisions
3. **Take decisions:** Meeting with Biometrics rep + Biometrics Head (DDO) + Data Office
4. **Update collection:** Data scope updated to reflect decisions

**Business Sensitivity Exception:**
- GPTs or DDOs may flag and exclude specific studies due to business sensitivity
- Must communicate exclusions to R&D Data Office
