## Key Points

- **EDSE:** External Data Sharing Ecosystem - umbrella term for all external data sharing aspects `[ref: slide 3]`
- **Challenge:** Need for clear roadmap, understanding of capabilities/roles, scalability, and alignment `[ref: slide 2]`
- **Status:** Strategic planning in progress

---

## The EDSE Challenge `[ref: slide 3]`

### Key Points:
- External Data Sharing is complex with multiple patterns, stakeholders, steps
- Data use growth increases need for AZ to share with vendors/collaborators
- Data Office role has extended into contracting and operational capabilities
- Changes in need, scale, organizations have occurred over time
- Overall holistic plan, roadmap, and role are **unclear**
- Gaps may exist in AZ R&D EDSE capabilities

---

## Two Horizons Approach `[ref: slides 4-5]`

### Horizon A - Short Term (Acute Needs)
**Focus:** Current issues, gaps, bottlenecks, near-term solutions

**Activities:**
1. **Current issues:** Identify biggest bottlenecks and risks
2. **Current work:** Map ongoing work to bottlenecks, understand gaps
3. **Near term plan:** Alleviate most pressing needs

### Horizon B - Long Term (Strategic)
**Focus:** Vision, strategy, roadmap, capability development

**Activities:**
1. **Vision and DO mission:** Agree overall vision and DO/EDE mission
2. **Identify EDSE Patterns:** Agree patterns, DO's role, target state
3. **Prioritize work:** Set priorities for pattern target states
4. **Design the solution:** Solution design and project charter

---

## Strategic Framework `[ref: slides 7-12]`

### Cascading Questions:
1. **Vision:** Can we agree a "north star" for EDSE?
2. **DO/EDE Mission:** Federated self-serve vs specialist services vs E2E delivery?
3. **Patterns:** What are the different high-level use cases?
4. **Target States:** What target state per pattern?
5. **RACI:** Who is responsible for what?
6. **Success Metrics:** How do we measure Data Office success?
7. **Solution Design:** What achieves the target state?

### Activities Mapped:
- Activity 1: Propose/agree vision and DO/EDE mission
- Activity 2: List patterns, describe target states, define RACI
- Activity 3: Agree priorities and timeline
- Activity 4: List solution components and delivery timeline
