## Product Overview `[ref: slides 1-3]`

### Problem Solved
- Legal delays (5+ days for input) slowing business
- Skilled teams buried in low-value tasks
- No headcount relief for legal teams
- Generic AI tools lack legal nuance and security

### Agent Types
| Agent | Function |
|-------|----------|
| **Contracting Agents** | Draft, Compare, Review contracts |
| **Compliance Agents** | Multi-Review, Legal Radar (monitors legal changes) |
| **Research Agents** | Custom Agents, Discovery |
| **Anonymization** | Pre-processing data protection |

### Training Sources `[ref: slide 4]`
1. Government & public sources (legislation, case law)
2. Law firm partnerships (anonymized data)
3. Publisher integrations (FromCounsel UK, Wolters Kluwer)
4. Client knowledge integration (SharePoint)

---

## Key Differentiators `[ref: slide 6]`

- **Agentic workspace:** End-to-end vs point solutions
- **Data security:** Full anonymization before processing; on-premise option
- **Legally-trained:** Not generic LLM
- **LLM-agnostic:** Benchmarks and selects best model per task
- **Certifications:** ISO, SOC 2 Type II

---

## Roadmap (Next 6 Months) `[ref: slide 7]`

1. **Copilot integration:** Microsoft Copilot embedding
2. **Knowledge Collections:** Curated legal knowledge for AI agents
3. **Legal Radar:** Monitor legal changes and impact analysis
4. **Contract Lifecycle Workflows:** Draft to signature to renewals
